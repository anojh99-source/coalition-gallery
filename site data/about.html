About.

Serious underground sounds.
No nonsense.
Curated by people
that get it.

Coalition is a London-based events collective bringing underground sounds to grassroots venues across the city. Across jungle, breakbeat, garage, drum & bass, hardcore, house, grime and dubstep — we're not about hype. We're about the music, the selectors, and the community built around both.

Our Breakaway residency at Dalston Den is a jungle and breakbeat-focused event, running consecutive Fridays through summer 2026. Residents and guest selectors from deep within the underground scene. No filler, no nonsense — just proper music from people who live it.
